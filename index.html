About VVITU-ACM

Who We Are

The VVITU Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) Student Chapter is a dynamic community of students passionate about computing and technology. We aim to foster innovation, learning, and professional growth among our members.

Our chapter organizes various technical events, workshops, and competitions throughout the year to enhance students' technical skills and prepare them for the challenges of the digital world.

About SPARDHA

SPARDHA is our annual technical fest that brings together students from across the country to showcase their technical prowess. With over 20 events spanning various domains of computer science and engineering, SPARDHA promises to be an unforgettable experience.
